Computer Lab

Computer Lab

The state of the art computer lab is really a treasure of modern technological innovations. The computer lab is designed with latest facilities of internet, software's, LAN setup with Latest computers, Multimedia, Printer etc. Ample space and time is provided to each individual student to become computer friendly and IT literate under the guidance of team of highly qualified , enthusiastic lecturers and teachers.

LIBRARY

LIBRARY

There is no friend as loyal as a book. A Library is like an island in the middle of the vast sea of ignorance. The school boast of its excellent and well set up library. It consist of a large hall filled with shelves, bookstands and furniture. There are over 5000 books in pour library. Each class is allotted one library period in the weekly timetable. Library is accessible to the students even during the summer vacations.

ATAL TINKERING LAB

Atal Tinkering Lab is a innovative center for kids to learn new things like Robotics, 3d designing and printing, programming. It is named after Former Prime Minister of India Atal Bihari Vajpayee.
